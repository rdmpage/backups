The Society's membership directory is now searchable. Use the suggested search tips below to maximize the effectiveness of your search.

Search Tips:

  • Search for last name only initially.
  • There is a limit of 15 returns per query, so choose search terms carefully.
  • For common names, such as "Jones" or "Smith," or names with spaces, such as "de Queiroz," include the first name of the person in the appropriate box.
  • Do not put quotes or other punctuation marks in or around your search string.
  • The search finds parts of names, so "Smith" will find "Smith," but also "Smithsonian"
